# Narra Training — Visual Asset Register

Tracks every course visual: type, sourcing route, rights, and accuracy sign-off.
**Rights rule:** prefer CC0 / CC BY / CC BY-SA / public domain (commercial use + attribution). **Updated 2026-07-05 (Andrew):** *limited* fair-use is now permitted where clean open licenses are unavailable — but it must be clearly labeled as fair-use with its real source, never dressed up as an open license. **Never fabricate a source/license.** Full per-image provenance lives in [`asset-credits.md`](asset-credits.md); nothing ships without human license-verification **and** Margie accuracy sign-off.

**Type key:** **A** = in-house diagram/chart/flowchart/table (original, on-brand, no third-party rights) · **B** = anatomical/procedural illustration (open-license/PD first, else SME-verified original; **accuracy sign-off required**) · **C** = clinical photograph (open-license/PD or Margarita's consented library; de-identify; **accuracy sign-off required**).

**Status:** `planned` → `sourced/drafted` → `license-verified` → `SME-signed-off` → `shipped`.
