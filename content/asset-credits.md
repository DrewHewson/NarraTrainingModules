## License posture (changed 2026-07-05, per Andrew)

The asset register's original rule was CC0/CC-BY/CC-BY-SA/PD only. Andrew has authorized **accepting limited fair-use** where clean open licenses are unavailable. Even so: (a) prefer PD/CC0/CC-BY wherever possible (all four above are), (b) any true fair-use item must be clearly labeled as such with its real source — never dressed up as an open license, and (c) attribution is displayed for every CC-BY/BY-SA/PD asset (shown as the figure credit line in the reading view).
